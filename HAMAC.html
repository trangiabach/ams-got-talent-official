General information

Virtual reality greatly attracts the attention of today's young people. Inspired by a movie, a group of students accidentally get caught up in a game, and they must fight to survive to the end. Will they escape or forever be stuck in this virtual world? As a martial arts club, HAMAC will recreate its dramatic performances and flexible movements on the stage of AGT XIII. Hopefully this dramatic but equally humorous performance will bring a bustling atmosphere to everyone.
