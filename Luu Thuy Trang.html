General information

Central Highlands, a land with the breath of greatness. Through two wars against the French and the Americans, the people of the Central Highlands wrote many epic versions of solidarity and heroic spirit against foreign invaders to gain freedom for the Fatherland. Please listen to the two works "Tieng chay tren soc Bom Bo" composed by Xuan Hong and "Hat mung anh hung Nup" by musician Tran Quy to feel the love expressed through the heroic history pages of the indomitable Central Highlands.
