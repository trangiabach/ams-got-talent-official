General information

Inspired by the short story "Melancholy on top of Puvan", Doi nhieu thi phi brings to AGT XIII a story about the journey of people from the city to the peak of Puvan to see the "sau" flowers, which is decorated with unique colors of mountainous regions and deep contemplations about life. Did God determine this fate to be dreadful? Please enjoy the performance of Doi nhieu thi phi to find out the answer!
